3. Idghaam الإِدْغَامُ (The Merging/Inserting) Note: There are two types of Idghaam that apply to Noon Saakinah and Tanween. Idghaam is when you merge the noon saakinah/tanween into any of the six letters of Idghaam such that the Idghaam letter is emphasised with Shaddah ( ّ ); while the Noon Saakinah/Tanween is not pronounced clearly. The Letters of Idghaam: ن و ل م ر ي
