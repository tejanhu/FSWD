The Light Letters The remaining letters are in total 19, which are always light letters: ب، ت، ث، ج، ح، د، ذ، ز، س، ش، ع، ف، ك، م، ن، ه، و، ء، ي They’re also known as the Lowered letters, because unlike the heavy letters; the back of our tongue is kept low and is not raised to the roof of the mouth when pronouncing them.
