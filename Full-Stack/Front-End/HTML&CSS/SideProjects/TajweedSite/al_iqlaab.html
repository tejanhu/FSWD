2. Iqlaab/Qalb الإِقْلَابُ/القَلْبُ (The Changing) This is when the noon saakinah/tanween comes immediately before the letter ب, so you change the noon saakinah/tanween into the letter م. How to pronounce the rule of Iqlaab: During recitation of Qur’aan, whenever you come across the letter Noon or a Tanween followed by the letter Baa, you will not pronounce them, rather you will change the noon sound into that of a Meem Sakinah with Ghunnah. Also you should not close your lips completely stressing them nor open them widely during pronunciation of this Meem, but you should make a small gap between your lips to give the sound of Ghunnah.
