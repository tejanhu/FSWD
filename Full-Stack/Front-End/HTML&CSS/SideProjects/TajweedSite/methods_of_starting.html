The Different types of Starting There are only two types of starting (الإِبْتِدَاءُ): (The Optional Start) الإِخْتِيَارِيّ: This is the starting that is out of choice which has three acceptable ways and one unacceptable way. (For your level, it is best to start from the beginning of a verse and stop based on the signs we learned above until you learn Arabic.) (The Examining Start) الإِخْتِبَارِي: This is the starting that occurs during an exam; when being tested where the Sheikh may ask you to start in a particular verse.
