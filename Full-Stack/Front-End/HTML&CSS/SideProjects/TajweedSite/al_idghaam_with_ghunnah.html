Idghaam With Ghunnah Idghaam With Ghunnah is also known as an “Incomplete Idghaam” due Ghunnah being a characteristic of noon 3. Idghaam إِدْغَامٌ بِغُنَّ (Merging/Inserting With Nasal Sound) This is when you merge the noon saakinah/tanween into any of the four letters of Idghaam such that the Idghaam letter is emphasised with shaddah ( ّ ) and prolonged for two “harakaat” vowels; while the noon saakinah/tanween sound is removed completely The Letters of Idghaam With Ghunnah: ن و م ي Examples: Note: This rule does not apply when Noon Saakinah/Tanween comes before the letters of Idghaam with Ghunnah in a single word.
