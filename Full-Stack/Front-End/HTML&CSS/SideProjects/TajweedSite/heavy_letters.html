Heavy Letters There are in total 7 heavy letters, which are also known as Elevated Letters because the back of our tongue is raised towards the roof of the mouth when pronouncing them.: خ، ص، ض، غ، ط، ق، ظ These 7 letters may be remembered with the phrase: خُصَّ ضَغْطٍ قِظْ Below are examples of words with these letters: خَلَقَ - خ صَالِحِيْنَ - ص ضَرَبَ - ض غَفَرَ - غ طَارِقْ - ط قَامَ - ق ظَلَمَ - ظْ Whenever you put a voweled alif i.e.: fatha, dhammah or kasrah before any of these letters and make it saakin, you should notice the back of your tongue being raised to the roof of your mouth: اَخْ اَصْ اَضْ اَغْ اَطْ اَقْ اَظْ Examples: خِيَانَةً تَخُوْنُوْا خَلَقَ خ صِبْغَةَ اللهِ قُصُوْرًا صَالِحِيْنَ ص ضِيَآءً ضُعَفَآءُ ضَرَبَ ض غِطَآءَكَ غُلِبَتِ غَفَرَ غ طِبَاقًا طُمِسَتْ طَعَامِكَ ط قِطَعٌ قُوْلُوْا قَامَ ق عَظِيْمٌ ظُلِمُوْا ظَعْنِكُمْ ظ
