Introduction

What is Tajweed?

Tajweed is giving every letter of the Qur’aan its due right and characteristics whilst observing the rules that apply to those letters when reciting the Qur’aan.

What is the reward of perfecting your Tajweed?

The prophet Peace and blessings be upon him said: (Indeed the one who recites the Qur’aan beautifully, smoothly, and precisely, will be in the company of the noble and obedient angels. As for the one who recites with difficulty, stammering or stumbling through its verses, then he will have twice that reward.)

Why learn Tajweed?

To recite the Qur’aan as close as possible to the way the prophet Muhammad Peace and blessings be upon him recited. The Prophet Muhammad Peace and blessings be upon him said: (the best of you are those who learn the Quran and teach it)
