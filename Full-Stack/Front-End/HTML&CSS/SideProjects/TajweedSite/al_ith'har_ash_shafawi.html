Al-Ith’har Ash-Shafawi الإِظْهَارُ الشَّفَوِي (To recite clear) This is when you pronounce the Meem Saakinah clearly without any form of merging or hiding when any of the remaining letters come immediately after Meem Saakinah. If waaw or fa comes after Meem Saakinah, one should emphasise reciting it clearly to avoid confusing the listener that a baa was recited. This rule will apply whether any of the following letters comes after Meem Saakinah in one word or two words.
